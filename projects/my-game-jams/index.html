My Game Jams

updated: 2025-03-01

Ludum Dare is a challenge to make a video game in 48 hours.

Each of these games made in 48 hrs SOLO.

  • Except for 2 Shoes :)

The THEME is assigned by the Ludum Dare, but you’ll see they all have their own .. theme?

Saving Greg

Released: April 2021
Theme: Deeper and deeper
Join Greg on his most harrowing adventure yet as he descends into the unknown.

2 Shoes

Released: October 2019
Theme: Start with nothing
A 2-socked Greg must find his way in a world where footwear is power.

Greg Island

Released: April 2018
Theme: Combine 2 Incompatible Genres

Survival horror meets dating sim in this genre-bending Greg adventure.

Gluttonous Greg

Released: December 2017
Theme: The more you have, the worse it is
Help Greg manage his insatiable appetite before it consumes him.

Don’t Die, Greg

Released: July 2017
Theme: Running out of power
Greg’s legendary first appearance
